# NexFit — Full UGC Ad Scripts

Three full scripts, each following Problem → Discovery → Solution → CTA. Each is written to be spoken aloud in 30-45 seconds, casual/first-person, camera-facing.



## Script 1: "Bulky vs Aesthetic" (Primary Ad)

**Hook:**
"Okay can we talk about how every gym just wants to make you bulky?"

**Problem:**
"I joined like three different gyms before this and every single trainer had the same plan — lift heavy, eat a ton, get big. But that's not what I wanted? I just wanted to look... toned. Lean. Put together. Not competition-ready."

**Discovery:**
"A friend of mine kept talking about this place called NexFit, and honestly I only went because she wouldn't stop mentioning it."

**Solution:**
"Turns out they actually build your program around the LOOK you want, not just numbers on a bar. My trainer literally asked me what physique I was going for before writing anything. First gym that's ever done that."

**Proof/Result:**
"Two months in and I finally look like I work out — not like I'm training for a strongman competition."

**CTA:**
"If you're tired of generic gym plans, just go check them out. Link's in my bio."



## Script 2: Busy Professional Angle

**Hook:**
"POV: you have a 9 to 5 and still want to look decent by the time summer hits."

**Problem:**
"I kept putting off the gym because I convinced myself I needed 2 hours a day to see any results. Spoiler — I do not have 2 hours a day."

**Discovery:**
"Found NexFit through Instagram actually, saw a Reel about their 45-minute aesthetic-focused sessions and I was like, okay, let's try it."

**Solution:**
"The sessions are quick but they're actually structured around building the look, not just 'burning calories' or whatever. In and out in 45 minutes, no wasted time."

**Proof/Result:**
"Been going 4 times a week for 6 weeks and I've noticed more change than an entire year at my old gym."

**CTA:**
"If you're short on time but still want real results, this is honestly worth checking out."



## Script 3: Skeptic-Turned-Convert

**Hook:**
"I was so against joining another gym after my last two memberships were a complete waste."

**Problem:**
"Every gym felt the same — random machines, no real plan, trainers who just say 'add more weight' and walk off."

**Discovery:**
"My roommate basically forced me to try NexFit with her. I went in expecting nothing."

**Solution:**
"But the first session, they actually sat down and asked what physique goals I had — like actually cared about the aesthetic, not just strength numbers. That's when I realized this was different."

**Proof/Result:**
"It's been 8 weeks. I don't even dread going anymore, which if you knew me before, is wild."

**CTA:**
"Not saying you have to join, just saying — if you've been let down by gyms before, give this one an actual shot."



**Note on tone:** All scripts avoid brand-speak ("premium," "revolutionary," "transform your life"). Every claim is framed as personal experience, not a brand promise — this is what keeps UGC ads feeling authentic instead of scripted.
